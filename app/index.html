What is portfolio rebalancing? What is Rebalance.in?

Rebalancing your portfolio is the process of buying or selling assets to make your portfolio's asset allocation match your desired allocation. Rebalance.in is a tool to calculate exactly how to rebalance.

Easy

Just enter in your assets, their current values, and your desired allocations. We'll do the rest!

Secure

All calculations are performed in your web browser. Your information is never transmitted to our servers.

Free

It's completely free. Because your money should stay in your portfolio.

Want to learn more about portfolios and portfolio rebalancing? Reddit's Personal Finance Subreddit is a great place to start.
